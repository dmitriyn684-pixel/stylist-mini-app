# DimkoFF Visual Brand Guideline 2026

## Identity statement

DimkoFF - SMM + AI Product Builder.

I turn business signals into digital products.

Я превращаю бизнес-сигналы в SMM-системы, AI-продукты и Telegram-воронки.

## Core method

SIGNAL -> SYSTEM -> PRODUCT -> GROWTH

- Signal: ниша, аудитория, боль, оффер, positioning и content signals.
- System: SMM strategy, content, Telegram funnel, lead magnet и analytics.
- Product: AI bot, Mini App, landing, backend, database и automation.
- Growth: launch, test, data, UX, retention и scale.

## Visual system

- Primary wordmark: DIMKOFF with signal dot.
- Compact mark: DFF monogram inside a rounded system container.
- Product mark: DFF / AI / SIGNAL.
- Foundation: Obsidian Black `#080A0F`, Graphite Carbon `#11151D`, Volcanic Black `#050608`.
- Primary signal: Signal Mint `#00FFD1`.
- Support: Deep Mint `#00BFA6`, Digital Blue `#5D8CFF`, Warm White `#F5F0E8`, Canyon Gray `#8D929C`, Growth Green `#8FD66B`.
- Premium accent only: Warm Gold `#D8B35E`.
- Typography: Arial Bold for display, Arial for body, Consolas for technical labels.
- Layout: 12-column poster grid, 54-point margins, large uppercase titles, low-contrast technical notes.

## Original pattern library

1. Signal Pattern - dots, impulses and horizontal signal traces.
2. System Pattern - brackets, braces, grid nodes and functional labels.
3. Product Pattern - interface blocks, cards and product nodes.
4. Growth Pattern - cycles, rising vectors and repeat arrows.
5. AI Grid - connected neural nodes with controlled density.
6. Telegram Flow - chat bubbles, TG nodes and conversation routes.
7. Funnel Map - attention -> trust -> lead -> product -> retention.

Keep patterns in the background, low contrast, so they support content and never fight the message.
